Quicken and Personal Finance

  • Quicken
    Helps you organize and track all your personal finances--accounts, credit cards, investments, budgets, and online banking--in one convenient place.

  • Quicken Financial Planner (QFP)
    While Quicken helps you track your finances, QFP helps you plan for the important events in your financial future, including college, retirement, and buying a home.

  • ExpensAble
    Your one-step solution to doing expense reports. ExpensAble automatically sorts your expenses, does all the math, and creates a detailed expense report for you. Works with Quicken and Pocket Quicken.

  • Pocket Quicken
    Instantly organizes your finances--anytime, anywhere. Easily update checking, credit card, savings, even cash accounts on-the-go. Then, send your data to Quicken or ExpensAble with no double-entry.

QuickBooks and Small Business Finance

  • QuickBooks Small Business Online
    The Web site from Intuit that is dedicated to small business people. It features QuickBooks news and information, a directory of links to other small business Web sites, and QuickBooks tips and updates.

  • QuickBooks
    QuickBooks is the fastest, easiest way to manage your business. It provides all the key business features you need, including invoicing, inventory, payroll and more. Try it free!

  • QuickBooks Pro
    QuickBooks Pro has all the accounting and payroll features of QuickBooks, plus time tracking, estimating, and job costing. It's project tracking and accounting all in one. Try it free!

  • QuickPay
    QuickPay is Intuit's payroll solution for Quicken business users. QuickPay saves you time because it automatically calculates earnings, taxes, and deductions for you.

TurboTax, MacInTax and Personal Taxes

  • TurboTax and MacInTax
    TurboTax and MacInTax, America's #1 tax software, make taxes easy. Just answer the simple questions and TurboTax fills out your tax return for you, like an accountant would. It's the easiest way to do your taxes.

ProSeries and Professional Tax Preparation

  • ProSeries Professional Tax Software
    ProSeries, the comprehensive yet easy-to-use software for professional tax preparers, includes productivity-boosting features such as expanding tables, unlimited forms and schedules, pick lists, customizable client letter and billing options, and more.

    The ProSeries family of software includes products in these categories: federal and state individual tax, federal and state business tax, electronic filing, research and reference, accounting and client software, as well as self-study courses and seminars.

Online Financial Services

  • Quicken Financial Network (QFN)
    Our new financial information and services area. From the new QFN home page, you can get stock quotes; read today's business news; browse the financial library; and visit the investments, insurance, and online banking centers.

  • Online banking
    Get daily data on your accounts directly from your bank, and then use it to update your Quicken registers automatically.

  • Online bill payment
    A convenient service that lets you pay bills online. You send payment instructions via modem, and payment is made automatically from your bank account to the payee.

  • NETworth
    A comprehensive mutual fund service. Search the Morningstar database of fund information, organize your mutual fund data with the Mutual Fund Market Manager, and update your portfolio with a stock quote service (15 minute delayed).

  • Investor Insight
    Lets you track your portfolio of stocks and mutual funds. Retrieve current news and analyst reports about your investments, and download the latest prices from the markets.
