If you're using Netscape 2.0, you can automatically add any of the items on your Netscape hotlist to your CheckList.

First you'll need to upload the file called "bookmarks.htm" from the Netscape directory on your computer. Once you've uploaded the file, CheckList will show you a list of the links in your bookmark file and let you choose which bookmarks you want to add to your CheckList.

Enter the full path to your bookmark file (or click on Browse... to find the file) then click on Upload File.
